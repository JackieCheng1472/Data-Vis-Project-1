Population distribution and GDP per capita

Made by: Jackie Cheng

Data Sources: All data from Our World In Data

Datasets used:

  • Gender Development Index vs GDP per Capita
  • Share of Urban and Rural Population

Years: Data ranges from 1960–2024. Use the year slider to explore different years. Default view shows 2023 as many countries do not have 2024 data.

Color choices: Blue = Urban, Orange = Rural, Pink = Gender Development Index. Countries are colored by world region in the scatterplot.
